2016 Winners

  • Human Capital

    The indivual or group that has made major contributions in the development or recruitment of IT talent to the Charlotte area.

    Winners

    • UNC Charlotte College of Computing and Informatics
    Finalists
    • Tech Talent South
    • TIAA-CREF

  • Student Innovator

    The individual student that has created new and innovative technology or has demonstrated the innovative use of existing technology to drive value.

    Winners

    • Tom Nguyen, Philip O Berry Academy of Technology
    Finalists
    • Scott Bowman, Charlotte Latin School
    • Jackson Styers/Lizbeth Rangel, Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools

    The entrepreneurial individual that has turned a creative vision into a profitable and enduring business through the direct application of information technology.

    Winners

    • Brian Kelly, CloudGenera
    Finalists
    • Keith Luedeman, goodmortgage.com
    • Mark Mahoney, Jackrabbit Technologies

    Finalists
    • AddShoppers
    • Dealcloud

    Finalists
    • SnapAV
    • Steritech

  • Business Value – Corporate

    The IT solution that creates the greatest business value, and the company where it was implemented.

    Winners

    • Ally Bank, New Online Banking Platform
    Finalists
    • Premier Inc, SOLR
    • Premier Inc, HVBP

    Winners

    • SIM Charlotte Region
    Finalists
    • Corporate Information Technology
    • North Highland Consulting

  • Cool Innovation

    The individual or company that demonstrates the best example of creativity, execution and applied invention, which addresses a new approach to an old problem that may make lasting and far reaching changes in the way we work with each other. Value + Creativity + Execution = Cool Innovation.

    Winners

    • Ekos Brewmaster
    Finalists
    • Charlotte Mecklenburg School
